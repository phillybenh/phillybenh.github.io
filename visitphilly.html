Parks

  • Philadelphia Museum of Art - The location of the “Rocky Steps”, the rocky statue, and one of the world's largest municipally owned art collections
  • Rodin Museum - Indoor/outdoor sculpture museum with the largest collection of Auguste Rodin's work outside of Paris
  • West Fairmount Park - Home to the Philadelphia Horticultural Center, central arboretum, and Shofuso Japanese house and Garden
  • Wissahickon Valley Park - Miles of hiking trails in the woods
  • FDR Park - Municipal park with lakes and views of the stadiums
  • Schuylkill Banks - Park with river and skyline views on the 60 mile Schuylkill River Trail
  • Rittenhouse Square, Washington Square, Franklin Square, Logan Square,Dilworth Park/City Hall - These downtown parks were originally laid out as five public squares by William Penn when he designed his settlement here in the 1680’s.
  • Race Street Pier - This pier was converted into a modern park with views of the Delaware River and the Ben Franklin Bridge

Breweries & Distilleries

  • Yards Brewing Company - One of philly’s largest craft breweries, just moved into a shiny new facility
  • Philadelphia Brewing Company - One of philly’s largest craft breweries
  • Second District Brewing Company - New brewpub in South Philly
  • Brewery ARS - New garage brewery in South Philly, usually has food trucks
  • Crime & Punishment Brewing Co. - Small brewpub in Brewerytown
  • Evil Genius Beer Company - Brewpub
  • Fermentary Form - Small brewery specialising in mixed culture and sour beers (check their social media for hours)
  • Tired Hands Fermentaria - Located in the western suburbs, this brewery and restaurant generates a lot of hype in the craft beer scene
  • Forest & Main Brewing Co. -Located in Ambler (northwest of Philly) this brewpub specializes in craft English- & Belgian-inspired beers
  • New Liberty Distillery - has small cocktail ber, and this distillery offers tours and tastings
  • Rowhouse Spirits - Craft distillery (located next to Philly Brewing)
  • Red Brick - Craft distillery specializing in whiskey and specialty liquor, contact for information on tours and tastings
  • Federal Distilling/Stateside Vodka - This distillery features a cocktail bar
  • Philadelphia Distilling - Offers craft cocktail and guided distillery tours
